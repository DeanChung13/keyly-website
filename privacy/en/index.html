Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

💡 Our Core Privacy Promise: We only take what we need to function, and we instantly discard your data after cloud processing. We never compromise your basic privacy.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

Keyly is committed to providing a secure typing experience. Our AI processing is divided into two modes:

A. Offline AI (On-Device Models)

If you utilize models supported by your device's built-in AI capabilities, all processing occurs completely offline on your device. Keyly absolutely does not transmit or store these operations.

B. Cloud AI Processing (Ephemeral)

To provide more powerful and precise text refinement, we offer features that connect to top-tier cloud models (such as the OpenAI API and Anthropic API). When your drafts are sent to the cloud, we strictly adhere to an "Ephemeral Processing" principle:

  • Your text acts only as a brief anonymous request. Once the AI result is retrieved, our servers do not store any input logs or conversation history.
  • We strictly require our partner providers to follow the highest data security standards, guaranteeing that your inputs are never used to train future AI models.

C. Why do we need "Allow Full Access"?

In the iOS ecosystem, third-party keyboards must be granted "Allow Full Access" permission to initiate network connections. This permission is required because Keyly must connect to Cloud AI to refine your text. Rest assured, we absolutely do not log your daily keystrokes or collect general typing content unrelated to Keyly's refinement features. Network connections only occur when you explicitly tap to process text with AI.

D. Accounts and Quota Verification

To calculate quotas for general users (daily limits) and identify Pro subscription users, the system assigns an anonymous identifier (accessToken). We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your real name, phone number, or address.

E. Analytics Data and Third-Party Tracking

To continuously improve your experience, this app utilizes data analytics tools such as Firebase:

  • We only track product conversion funnels, basic click events, and crash reports (Crashlytics).
  • These analytics SDKs absolutely do not contain any of your keyboard text inputs.

2. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell any of your data to advertisers. Your necessary data is only communicated via brief encrypted connections with trusted third-party infrastructure (such as Cloud AI providers) during the delivery of AI text processing or subscription services.

3. Your Privacy Rights

You may revoke Keyly's "Full Access" permission at any time in iOS under Settings > General > Keyboard. Once revoked, cloud connection features will become unavailable. If you have any privacy-related questions or wish to delete your account, please feel free to contact us via our App Store developer support page.

4. Policy Changes

Future updates may include changes to this policy. In the event of material or substantial changes, we will notify you on our official website's homepage and within the app.
