Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

💡 Our core promise: We only use what is necessary to provide the service, discard cloud-processing data immediately afterward, and never touch your ordinary private typing beyond that scope.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

Keyly is designed to provide a typing experience that feels both useful and safe. Our AI processing operates in two modes:

A. Offline AI (On-Device Models)

If your device supports on-device AI features, processing happens entirely offline on your device. Keyly does not transmit or store these requests.

B. Cloud AI Processing (Burn After Reading)

To provide stronger and more precise refinement, we offer access to top-tier cloud models such as the OpenAI API and Anthropic API. When your draft is sent to the cloud, we follow a strict burn-after-reading principle:

  • Your text is handled only as a brief anonymous request. Once the refined result is returned, our servers do not retain the input log or conversation history.
  • We require our infrastructure partners to follow high data-security standards and not use your input to train future AI models.

C. Why do we need “Allow Full Access”?

Within iOS, third-party keyboards need the “Allow Full Access” permission in order to make network connections. Keyly needs that permission only to connect to cloud AI services when you ask for advanced refinement. Rest assured, we do not log your everyday keystrokes or collect general typing content unrelated to Keyly features. A network request is made only when you explicitly trigger an AI action.

If you choose not to enable Full Access: you can still use Keyly's core Zhuyin engine and regular typing features. If your device supports local AI, basic text correction can still run fully offline with your privacy staying on-device.

D. Account and Quota Verification

To distinguish standard users with daily limits from Pro subscribers, the system issues an anonymous identifier (accessToken) for quota checks. We do not collect real-name identity data such as your legal name, phone number, or home address.

E. Analytics Data and Third-Party Tracking

To keep improving the experience, the app uses analytics tools such as Firebase:

  • We track product funnel conversion, basic click events, and crash reporting through Crashlytics.
  • These analytics tools do not include your keyboard text input.

2. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your data to advertisers. Data is shared only in brief encrypted communications with trusted third-party infrastructure when required to provide AI text processing or subscription services.

3. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for children under 13 years old, or under the applicable age of digital consent in your region. We do not knowingly collect children's personal information. If you believe we have done so unintentionally, please contact us and we will delete it.

4. Your Privacy Rights and Account Management

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data where applicable.

  • Revoking permission: You may disable Keyly's Full Access permission at any time in iOS under Settings > General > Keyboard.
  • Deleting your account: If you created an account using Sign in with Apple, choosing “Delete Account” in the app will deactivate your anonymous identifier and remove the Apple ID linkage from our side.

Important: deleting your account does not cancel your subscription. Because billing is handled by Apple, you must cancel through App Store subscription management to stop future auto-renewal charges. For privacy questions, contact us at support@keylyapp.com.

5. Policy Changes

Future releases may update this policy. If we make a material change, we will notify you on our website homepage and inside the app.

GDPR note: For users in the European Union and the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing relevant data is the performance of a contract, meaning the delivery of keyboard input and AI refinement services you requested.
